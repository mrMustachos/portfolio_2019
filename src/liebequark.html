While working at Reason Media I developed the website for a new brand of Bavarian style quark that was in the process of trying to break into the US market. Like yogurt, but better. Milder and creamier. Rich and velvety. Starting with our authentic Bavarian recipe, quark celebrates a delicious marriage of yogurt and cream cheese. The light fun nature of both the quark and the brand lead me to develop a number of animations and scroll transitions with Greensock and Scrollmagic javascript libraries that I sprinkled throughout the site. Other tech used it the development and deployment of the site was parcel.js for the build and dev process and Wordpress and the CMS for the client.
