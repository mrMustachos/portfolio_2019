The HBO Call Center would run quarterly sweepstakes for their employees as a way of creating publicity for a specific show, as well as influencing their employees to sell more subscriptions for HBO. Employees would visit the sweepstakes page daily and sign up for their chance to win show-inspired prizes. The designs of the sweepstakes were based off of promotional materials such as window clings and standees created from the shows upcoming keyart.
