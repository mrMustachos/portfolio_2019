The content for this book was created from my daily observations at my internship with Puma as well as photos that I took of the office and events I attended. The book is largely made of journal entries and class reflections on the graphic design field as a whole as well as the daily struggles and triumphs that come with being a fulltime intern. The goals in laying out this book was to stylistically adhere to Puma's style guidelines. The writing within my entries, as well as the layout of the book was the final project for my internship class.
