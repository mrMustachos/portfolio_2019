Here are examples of two artistic approaches to an assignment for a course taken in the spring of my senior year. In conjunction with the Model United Nations Club at Endicott College, we were to design posters exemplifying how mid-century Norway reacted to potential communist advances into South Korea. The posters on the right were created to mimic the design, style and form of the 1950â€™s; the posters on the left were designed with more current design principles in mind. The use of iconic Soviet imagery and color were used to evoke the feeling of control and aggression that were associated with Communism in this time period.
