During my time at Songkick the name of the game was helping artist sell tickets directly to there fans. Our mission was to help the artist connect directly with their fans by selling tickets to there shows through there own website via an iframe controlled by Songkick. Along with designing and developing the stand alone pages where the tour listing lived my primary goal was creating bespoke styled stores to match the look and feel of the artist site.
