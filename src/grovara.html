While working at Reason Media I developed the website for a company called Grovara. Grovara has standardized product, order, and shipping information for brands and buyers to navigate the complexities of international export. They work with partners in different sectors and redefining what it means to help brands go global and buyers accessing U.S. brands. The focal point of this site was there “Go Global” page. Grovara’s pitch is that they can help take a brand Global in 120 days. This parallax style timeline walks potential clients through how Grovara can help there brand grow and succeed in a global market.
